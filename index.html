About Islam

Basic Beliefs

Muslims believe in one God (Allah), the angels, the revealed books, the prophets, the Day of Judgment, and divine decree.

The testimony of faith (Shahada) states: "There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is the messenger of Allah."

Five Pillars of Islam

  1. Shahada (Declaration of Faith)
  2. Salah (Prayer five times daily)
  3. Zakat (Charity to the needy)
  4. Sawm (Fasting in Ramadan)
  5. Hajj (Pilgrimage to Mecca)

History of Islam

Islam began in 7th century Arabia with the revelation of the Quran to Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Today, Islam is the world's second-largest religion with over 1.8 billion followers from diverse cultures and backgrounds.
